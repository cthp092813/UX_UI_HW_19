About Me

I am a Vietnamese American UX/UI designer interested in working in the gaming industry.

I previously worked as a registered nurse (RN) for a year, and while I loved taking care of others, it didn't quite satisfy my creative self. During my career transition, I ventured to learn more about the tech industry and briefly learned about HTML and CSS from Codeacademy for Full Web Development. I then stumbled upon UX/UI design when taking a career test, signed up for a University of Denver's UX/UI Design Bootcamp, and I have fallen in love with it ever since!

What I love about design is that it does not have a "correct" or "right" answer. I get to synthesize a multitude of solutions, both terrible and maybe even genius, and test them. I love building rapport with clients and view them as part of the team to enhance the user experience for the product.

My UX/UI Skills

User Research

I figure out the thought processes and pain points of users and stakeholder through research methods like qualitative user interviews.

Tool: Google Drive

User Empathy

To display users/stakeholders needs, I create a user persona, empathy map, and/or a user journey map, so others are able to understand their thought processes and pain points. Their needs become my main focus throughout the project.

Tools: Miro, Figma, Google Drive

Qualitative Interviews

I create an interview plan prior to my interviews. During my interviews, I like to ensure to my participants that no answer is a wrong answer so that the user experience I create is authentic and better for them.

Tool: Google Drive

Usability Testing

I create a usability test plan prior to conducting testing. During testing, I like to incorporate Steve Krug’s Usability Test Script to understand participant’s first impression of the product.

Tool: Google Drive

Information Architecture

When working on the IA of a product, I like to focus on who our user is and tailor the IA to the target audience. Card sorting and site mapping are the 2 techniques/skills I utilize for IA.

Tools: Miro, Figma

Wireframing/ Prototyping

I like to develop products from a bottom to top approach, so I would start with wireframing or the layout of the product and then build upon that to create the desired prototype.

Tool: Figma

Front End Development

I have proficient knowledge and understanding in HTML/CSS, and JavaScript/jQuery. I utilize these languages to bring my designs into reality!

Languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery

Tools: VS Code, Github
